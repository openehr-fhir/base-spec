### Introduction
This publication contains information to support FHIR tools working with openEHR content.
Specifically, it contains a FHIR representation of the openEHR reference model.
In general, this IG is a technical internal artifact to the tools; it's not anticipated
that the implementers who use either openEHR or FHIR will need to look at this. (hopefully)
